Certainly! Here's a table of common breakpoints:

| **Device/Screen Size** | **Breakpoint**   |
|------------------------|------------------|
| **Mobile (portrait)**  | `max-width: 599px` |
| **Mobile (landscape)** | `min-width: 600px` |
| **Tablet (portrait)**  | `min-width: 600px` to `max-width: 899px` |
| **Tablet (landscape)** | `min-width: 900px` |
| **Small Desktop**      | `min-width: 1024px` |
| **Large Desktop**      | `min-width: 1200px` |
